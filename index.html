To make a quick cake you need

  • Bread: you can pick any bread
  • Cream Cheese : wipped chream cheese for extra softness
  • Preserves: Any Jam or Preserve will work like strawberry, apricot, etc
  • Butter: why not
  • Box of icing (optional)

    • Preparation

  • Remove the bread crust from the sides
  • Apply thin layer of butter to each slice
  • Use 6 slices of crustless bread
  • apply preserve and cream cheese to each slice and start layering
  • once the last slice is left just cover one side and place on the stack
  • use frosting to decorate the cake
  • place a candle and happy (what ever you are celebrating)

    • There are many ways you can do the presentation of this cake

  • 1. Icing
  • 2. Using cookie cutter for fancy shapes
  • Use fresh fruit to add a layer between bread slices or decorate outer layer
  • 3. Decorate with a candied fruit (be creative )

    • Other Substitutions

    If you dont like preserves you can always use nutella(Chocolate spreads) and chocolate syrup with some powdered sugar
